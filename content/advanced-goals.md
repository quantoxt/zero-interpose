### ✅ **1. Built-in Direct Sales with Instant Payouts**
- **What**: Sell eBooks, print-on-demand (via API like Printful), or bundles **directly on their site**.
- **Why authors love it**:  
  - Keep **100% revenue** (minus payment processor fee).  
  - No Amazon/KDP lock-in.  
  - Use **Stripe/Paystack** with near-instant settlement (e.g., daily payouts).  
- **Your edge**: No third-party storefront—clean, branded, fast.

---

### ✵ **2. Reader Membership & Subscription Tiers**
- **What**: Let authors offer **paid newsletters, early access, or bonus content** behind role-based logins.
- **Why authors love it**:  
  - Recurring revenue (like Patreon, but **on their own domain**).  
  - Full ownership of subscriber data.  
- **Your edge**: You already work with **role-based access control**—perfect for “Free,” “Supporter,” and “VIP” tiers.

---

### ✵ **3. Personalized Reader Dashboards**
- **What**: Readers log in to see **their purchased books, reading progress, notes, or discussion threads**.
- **Why authors love it**:  
  - Builds **sticky, loyal communities**.  
  - Feels like “their own Substack + Kindle” hybrid.  
- **Your edge**: Vue + Nuxt handles dynamic user views beautifully.

---

### ✵ **4. Automated Launch & Pre-Order Campaigns**
- **What**: Built-in tools to **collect emails, take pre-orders, and auto-deliver books on launch day**.
- **Why authors love it**:  
  - No Mailchimp + Gumroad + WordPress juggling.  
  - All data stays **in their system**.  
- **Your edge**: Use serverless functions (Vercel Edge) for email triggers + PDF delivery.

---

### ✵ **5. Transparent, Real-Time Earnings Dashboard**
- **What**: Authors see **live sales, top readers, geographic heatmaps, and payout status**.
- **Why authors love it**:  
  - No more guessing or waiting 90 days.  
  - Feels like “my own KDP—but honest.”  
- **Your edge**: Clean charts with Chart.js or lightweight alternatives (no heavy analytics bloat).

---

### ✵ **6. Integrated “Ask Me Anything” (AMA) or Live Q&A**
- **What**: Schedule text/audio AMAs with readers—**no YouTube or Discord needed**.
- **Why authors love it**:  
  - Deepens connection without platform dependency.  
  - Can be gated for paying readers only.  
- **Your edge**: Use simple WebSocket or server-sent events (SSE) for real-time—no over-engineering.

---

### ✵ **7. Book-Specific Landing Pages (with SEO)**
- **What**: Auto-generated, SEO-optimized pages for **each book**—title, excerpt, reviews, buy button.
- **Why authors love it**:  
  - Drives organic traffic for *each* title (not just their homepage).  
  - Easy to share on socials.  
- **Your edge**: Nuxt’s static generation + dynamic routes = perfect for this.

---

### ✵ **8. Reader Reviews & Testimonials (Moderated by Author)**
- **What**: A clean section for **verified buyers to leave reviews**—no fake Amazon bots.
- **Why authors love it**:  
  - Social proof **they control**.  
  - Builds trust without platform manipulation.  
- **Your edge**: Simple form + approval workflow (no spam, no chaos).

---

### ✵ **9. “Behind the Book” Mini-Blogs or Journals**
- **What**: Authors share writing process, research, or deleted scenes **linked to each book**.
- **Why authors love it**:  
  - Adds depth without cluttering main site.  
  - Encourages repeat visits.  
- **Your edge**: Headless CMS (like Contentful or even Markdown files) + Nuxt content module.

---

### ✵ **10. One-Click Export of Reader Data**
- **What**: Authors can **download their entire email list, sales log, or reader notes** anytime.
- **Why authors love it**:  
  - True ownership = no vendor lock-in, ever.  
  - Peace of mind.  
- **Your edge**: Build as a core principle—**data portability = trust**.
